Buffalo Chicken Quesadillas

Description

Tangy barbeque sauce, tender shredded chicken, melty cheese, all wrapped up in a crispy tortilla. If that doesn't get your mouth watering I don't know what will. Read on to see how you can make these delicious quesadillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 3 tablespoons buffalo wing sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 10 medium-size tortillas
  • 1 1/2 cups each of cheddar and Monterey jack cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. In a medium skillet add olive oil and onion and saute for 5 minutes until onion is translucent. Add garlic and salt.
  2. Cook for an additional minute, stirring, until garlic is fragrant.
  3. Remove the skillet from stove and add chicken, buffalo sauce, ranch, cilantro, lime juice, and pepper. Mix to combine.
  4. Heat another medium skillet over medium heat.
  5. Place a tortilla in skillet and add a little of each cheese over one half of the tortilla.
  6. Fill the other side with the chicken mixture and fold the other cheese half over top of the chicken side.
  7. Heat just until the tortilla is golden brown on bottom. Carefully flip over and heat the other side the same.
  8. Serve warm and top with favorite toppings.
