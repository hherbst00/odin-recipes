Beef and Broccoli

Description

We all love Chinese takeout. So bring the takeout home to you with this easy and delicious recipe for Beef and Broccoli. It is a one pan recipe that's simple and doesn't break the bank.

Ingredients

For the meat

  • 1 pound London broil steak (thinly sliced in small strips)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound of broccoli cut into florets
  • 2 teaspoons sesame seeds for garnish

For the sauce

  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 6 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons corn starch
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 teaspoons ginger paste or fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2teaspoons black pepper

Steps

  1. If serving with rice, start cooking according to package directions.
  2. Slice steak into small, thin strips.
  3. In a small bowl, combine sauce ingredients , mix well, and set aside.
  4. Add one tablespoon of oil to a large skillet and heat on medium heat.
  5. Add broccoli and two tablespoons of water to skillet. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes or until broccoli is bright green.
  6. Set aside broccoli and add another tablespoon of oil to skillet. Add strips of steak and cook about 2 minutes per side.
  7. Pour sauce over meat and simmer on low for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Add broccoli and toss to coat.
