Shrimp Scampi

Description

Easy and restaurant quality. Eat juicy shrimp in a garlic and lemon butter sauce at home. Serve with toasted bread to soak up the sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound linguine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsely

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, add a teaspoon of salt and cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Carefully remove 1/2 cup of the pasta water and save before draining
  3. Thoroughly pat the shrimp dry with a paper towel.
  4. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  5. Add the shrimp to the heated pan and saute for 2 minutes.
  6. Flip shrimp over and cook an additional minute.
  7. Add the butter and garlic to the pan, stir until butter has melted and garlic is fragrant.
  8. Add the wine, lemon juice, salt,and red pepper flakes and bring sauce to a low simmer.
  9. Add pasta into shrimp and sauce and stir to coat well.
  10. Garnish with parsely.
